About Bookworms

Oxford Library

The Oxford Bookworms Library offers a wide range of original and adapted stories, both classic and modern, which take learners from elementary to advanced level through six carefully graded language stages:

  • Stage 1(400 headwords)
  • Stage 2(700 headwords)
  • Stage 3(1000 headwords)
  • Stage 4(1400 headwords)
  • Stage 5(1800 headwords)
  • Stage 6(2500 headwords)

Mora than fifty titles are also available on cassette, and there are many titles at Stages 1 to 4 which are specially recommended for younger learners. In addition to the introductions and activities in each Bookworm, resource material includes photocopiable test worksheets and Teacher's Handbooks, which contain advice on running a class library and using cassettes, and the answers for the activities in the books.
